Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a traditional Italian dish made with layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, and creamy bechamel or cheese.

It's baked to perfection, resulting in a hearty, comforting meal.

Ingredients:

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef or sausage
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato sauce
  • Tomato paste
  • Olive oil
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Egg
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Italian seasoning (basil, oregano)

Steps to Cook Lasagna:

  1. Boil the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a pan, heat olive oil and sauté chopped onions and garlic. Add ground beef and cook until browned.
  3. Stir in tomato sauce, tomato paste, salt, pepper, and Italian seasoning. Simmer the sauce for 15–20 minutes.
  4. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese with an egg and a bit of salt.
  5. In a baking dish, layer noodles, meat sauce, ricotta mixture, and mozzarella cheese. Repeat layers, finishing with sauce and a topping of mozzarella and Parmesan.
  6. Cover with foil and bake at 180°C (350°F) for 25–30 minutes. Remove foil and bake another 10–15 minutes until golden and bubbly.
  7. Let it rest for 10–15 minutes before slicing and serving.
