cheeseburger

Description

A cheeseburger is a juicy grilled beef patty served in a toasted bun and topped with melted cheese.

It’s often garnished with lettuce, tomato, onions, and condiments for extra flavor.

Ingredients:

  • Ground beef (for patties)
  • Salt and pepper
  • Cheese slices (e.g., cheddar)
  • Burger buns
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato slices
  • Onion slices
  • Pickles (optional)
  • Ketchup, mustard, or mayonnaise
  • Butter or oil (for toasting buns)

Steps to Make a Cheeseburger:

  1. Form the ground beef into patties and season both sides with salt and pepper.
  2. Cook the patties on a grill or skillet over medium-high heat for 3–4 minutes per side.
  3. In the last minute of cooking, place a slice of cheese on each patty to melt.
  4. Toast the burger buns lightly on the grill or pan.
  5. Assemble the burger: place the patty on the bun and add lettuce, tomato, onions, and your favorite condiments.
  6. Top with the other half of the bun and serve warm.
