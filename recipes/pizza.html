Pizza

Description

Pizza is a popular Italian dish featuring a flatbread crust topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and a variety of toppings.

It’s baked until the crust is crisp and the cheese is bubbly and golden.

Ingredients:

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic (optional)
  • Dried oregano or Italian seasoning
  • Salt
  • Toppings of choice;
    • Pepperoni
    • Mushrooms
    • Bell peppers
    • Onions
    • Olives, etc.

Steps to Cook Pizza:

  1. Preheat your oven to around 220°C (425°F).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Transfer the dough to a baking sheet or pizza stone and brush with olive oil.
  4. Spread a layer of tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  5. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese over the sauce, then add your chosen toppings.
  6. Bake for 12–15 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted and bubbling.
  7. Remove from the oven, let it cool slightly, slice, and serve.
